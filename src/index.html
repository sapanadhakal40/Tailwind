About Us

At Star Woodshop, we create high-quality, handcrafted furniture designed to elevate any space. Our passion for craftsmanship and attention to detail ensures each piece is both beautiful and built to last. From custom woodwork to timeless collections, we bring style and functionality together.

Our History

Founded in 2000, Star Woodshop started as a small family business with a deep love for woodworking. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name in furniture, known for our commitment to quality and craftsmanship

Our Mission

Our mission is to craft beautiful, durable furniture that brings comfort and style to your home. We prioritize sustainability and customer satisfaction, creating pieces that are meant to be cherished for years to come.
